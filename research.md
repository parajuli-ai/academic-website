## Research Focus

Advancing artificial intelligence through the convergence of **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Autonomous Agent Systems**, and **Computational Biology**. My work bridges the gap between theoretical deep learning and high-impact applications in healthcare, environmental intelligence, and automated reasoning.

## Primary Areas

### LLMs & Retrieval Systems (RAG)

Designing robust systems for knowledge retrieval and reasoning over complex data.

**Focus Areas**:
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) & Context-Augmented Generation (CAG)
- Hybrid Retrieval Systems (BM25 + Dense Vector Embeddings)
- Reasoning capabilities of Large Language Models
- Text standardization and validation using LLMs

**Recent Work**:

Integrating vision and language for comprehensive understanding of real-world environments.
